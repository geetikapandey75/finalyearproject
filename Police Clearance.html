What is a Police Clearance Certificate (PCC)?

A Police Clearance Certificate (PCC) is an official document issued by the police department, verifying that the individual has no criminal record or pending cases. It is often required for employment, immigration, visa, or residence purposes.

Documents Required

  • Passport-size photograph
  • Valid ID proof (Aadhaar Card, Passport, Voter ID, etc.)
  • Address proof (electricity bill, rent agreement, etc.)
  • Purpose letter (for job/visa/education etc.)
  • Any previous PCC (if applicable)

Application Process

  1. Fill out the online form below with accurate personal details.
  2. Upload the required ID and address proof documents.
  3. Submit your application for verification.
  4. The concerned police station will conduct background verification.
  5. You will receive your certificate via email or at your nearest police office.
