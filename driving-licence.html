Eligibility & Rules

  • Minimum age: 18 years for non-transport vehicles.
  • Must possess a valid learner's licence.
  • Medically fit to drive.
  • Knowledge of traffic rules and road safety.
  • Proof of age (birth certificate, school certificate, passport, etc.)
  • Proof of address (Aadhaar card, voter ID, utility bills, etc.)
  • Passport-size photographs.
