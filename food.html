Eligibility & Requirements

  • All food business operators must register under FSSAI.
  • Businesses with turnover above ₹12 lakh require a state licence.
  • Large-scale operators or importers require a central licence.
  • Compliance with food safety standards and hygiene is mandatory.
  • Valid ID proof and address proof of the owner are required.
  • Proof of business premises such as rent agreement or utility bill.
  • Passport-size photographs and NOC from local authorities.
