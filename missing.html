What To Do First

If someone you know goes missing, follow these essential steps:

  1. Call 112 immediately and report the missing person.
  2. Gather a recent photo, description, and last known location.
  3. Notify close friends, neighbors, and relatives.
  4. Submit a missing report on this portal.
  5. Share the poster on WhatsApp and social media.
  6. Check CCTV in nearby areas if possible.
  7. Join search teams and track live updates from volunteers.

Safety Alerts & Tips

  • Share your live location with trusted contacts.
  • Avoid isolated areas at night.
  • Use emergency SOS on your phone.
  • Teach children how to identify safe adults.
  • Report suspicious activity immediately.
