How to Apply

Step 1

Register on the official Passport Seva portal.

Step 2

Fill in the application form and upload required documents.

Step 3

Pay the applicable fee and schedule an appointment.

Step 4

Visit the Passport Seva Kendra (PSK) with original documents.

Documents Required

  • Proof of Date of Birth (Birth Certificate, 10th Marksheet, etc.)
  • Proof of Address (Aadhaar, Voter ID, Electricity Bill, etc.)
  • Proof of Nationality (PAN Card, Aadhaar, or Voter ID)
  • Old Passport (if applying for renewal)
  • Passport-size Photographs (as per official guidelines)

Police Verification Preparation

Prepare for the police verification step of your passport application. This ensures a smooth verification process at your residence.

  • What police will verify: Identity, address, and background details as provided in your application.
  • Questions they might ask: Employment details, family members, neighbors, and purpose of passport.
  • Documents to keep ready at home: Original proof of address, proof of identity, and supporting documents for verification.
  • Neighbor verification requirements: Neighbors may be asked to confirm your residence and identity.
  • Landlord NOC: If you are renting, keep a No Objection Certificate ready from your landlord.
  • Timeline expectations: Police verification usually completes within 7‚Äì15 days depending on the area.
  • How to handle verification visit: Be polite, answer all questions accurately, and provide documents when requested.
