Guidelines

  • Mandatory for all commercial establishments before starting operations.
  • Issued by the Municipal Corporation or local authority.
  • Licence type depends on the business category.
  • Valid for one year; renewal required before expiry.
  • Operations must not cause pollution, hazards, or nuisance.
  • Location must comply with zoning laws.
  • Authorities may inspect premises.
  • Operating without a valid licence can result in fines.
